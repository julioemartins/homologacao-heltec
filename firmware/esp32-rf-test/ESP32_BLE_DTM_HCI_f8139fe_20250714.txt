ESP32_BLE_DTM_HCI_f8139fe_20250714.bin， For DTM/BLE Signaling Test, Optimized BLE RX 2480
======================================================

Download Bin and Address（烧录固件地址）
ESP32_BLE_DTM_HCI_f8139fe_20250714.bin	0x1000
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Setup(按照下面 UART1 接线)
-------------------------------------------
ESP Launcher UART1 Connections （如果使用 ESP 串口板）:
UART1 RX - GPIO5
UART1 TX - GPIO4
GND - GND
------------------------------------------

RS232 UART1 Connections （如果使用通用 TTL/RS232 串口板）:
UART1 RX - GPIO4
UART1 TX - GPIO5
GND - GND
-----------------------------------------
BLE DTM auto init after power on the module, then you can start the DTM test. 
上电以上述 GPIO 作为 UART1 测试管脚自动初始化完成，默认 BLE 功率 6dBm，暂不支持 UART1 参数调整

CMW500 等仪器设置如下： 
RF Test -- EUT Control
HW Interface: USB to RS232 adapter
EUT Comm Protocol: HCI
Virtual COM Port: The comport of UART1(USB Cable Connect to CMW500)
Baud Rate: 115200
Protocol:	None
Auto Ranging: Selected



-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
